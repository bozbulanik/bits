                    1. INTRODUCTION

1.1 Purpose

     The Bits App is a personal knowledge management system. 
     It enables users to log, refine, and structure their thoughts 
     over time in a seamless and fluid interface. The system supports 
     ephemeral input, structured refinement, contextual metadata, and 
     intelligent organization.
     
1.2 Audience

     Primarily designed for personal use, with potential for 
     extension into a customizable tool for broader user bases.
